With Noorani Primer, you will start by learning individual Arabic letters, then move on to joining letters into words, and finally, you'll be able to read complete sentences fluently. You will also learn short vowels (Fatha, Kasra, Damma), elongation rules (Madd), and pronunciation techniques that ensure you recite the Quran correctly and beautifully.

Individual Letters
نورانی قاعدہ

Learn the basic Arabic letters, their shapes, and pronunciation.

Compound Letters
مُرَکَّبات

Understand how two or more letters join together to form words.

Long Vowel Letters
حَرفِ مَدہ

Master the long vowel sounds (Madd letters) for proper elongation.

Soft Letters
حَرفِ لین

Recognize soft-sounding letters like و and ی for smooth recitation.

Scilent Letters
سُکُون

Learn the silent letters (ْ) and how they connect in words.

Stressed Letters
تَشدِید

Practice stressed letters (ّ) and their strong pronunciation.

Double Vowels
تَنوِین

Understand the special double vowels (ــً، ــٌ، ــٍ) for fluency.

Rules of Noon Sakinah & Tanween
نُونِ ساکِن اور تَنوِین کے قَوَاعِد

Learn how to correctly pronounce noon sakinah (نْ) with tajweed rules.
